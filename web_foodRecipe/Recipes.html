Level: Easy
Preparation time: 10 mins
Cooking time: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • 1large bunch basil
  • 1large bunch parsley
  • 1kg new potatoes
  • larger potatoes halved
  • 100ml extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 small garlic clove

Step

  1. Preheat your oven to 200Â°C
  2. Chop the potatoes into chunks
  3. Put them on a baking tray with salt, pepper & about 1/4 cup olive oil
  4. Mix them until well coated, then bake in the oven for 30 mins
  5. Roast until golden
  6. Chop up the herbs until fine
  7. Add them to a big bowl, then add herbs, salt and pepper to taste, the mustard and another 1/4 cup olive oil
  8. Enjoy!
